# 📋 Clipper - Manage your clipboard address history efficiently

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Clipper-blue)](https://github.com/Blondevulpesmacrotis547/Clipper/releases)

Clipper is a tool designed to help you track and manage cryptocurrency addresses on your computer. It watches your clipboard for signals related to digital coins and keeps an organized history of the addresses you copy. This helps you verify data before you initiate transfers or store records. The software works for major coins like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Tron, Litecoin, and Dogecoin.
